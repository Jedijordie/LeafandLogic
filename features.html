Product Feature Information

Customizable Climate Control

| Easily control the climate for your plants!

    Our Leaf & Logic Smart Greenhouse offers an innovative climate control system, specifically designed for plants with similar environmental needs. Due to the compact size of our greenhouse, we've optimized conditions to cater to a select group of plants that thrive under the same temperature and humidity settings. This focused approach ensures that your plants receive the consistent care they require for optimal growth within a smaller, more manageable ecosystem.


  • With our Smart Greenhouse you can control irrigation/ soil moisture, LED light patterns, tempurature and musical stimulation for your plants.
  • Temperature adjusts automatically for optimal plant health.
  • Irrigation system waters plants when soil moisture is low.
  • LED lights are preset with optimal duration for plant type.
  • Turns on music daily for your plants to help with plant stimulation.

Intelligent Watering System

| Automatic watering system

The smart greenhouse is equipped with an intelligent watering system that uses capacitive soil moisture sensors to monitor the moisture levels in real time. Once the soil reaches the preset dryness threshold, the system initiates a watering cycle to hydrate the plants efficiently. This automated process ensures that your plants receive water only when necessary, reducing waste and promoting healthier root development.


  • With our Smart Greenhouse you can control irrigation through monitoring the soil moisture of your plants.
  • Sensors monitor soil moisture, watering your plants precisely when needed.
  • Automatically water plants when soil moisture is low.
  • Watering system will turn off when soil moisture is optimal.
  • Our intelligent watering system is set up to ensure the soil moisture is at an optimal level.

    Tempurature Control

    | Control the tempurature of your greenhouse

      Maintaining the perfect climate for plant health is crucial, and our Leaf & Logic Smart Greenhouse excels at this with precision temperature control. Embedded sensors continuously monitor the ambient temperature, relaying data in real-time to our intuitive app. The app is programmed with the optimal temperature range for your specific plants, ensuring they always enjoy conditions that mimic their natural habitats. When temperatures rise above the ideal, the system automatically activates an integrated fan to circulate air and reduce heat, safeguarding your plants from the stress of overheating. This responsive system operates seamlessly, offering peace of mind that your greenery is nurtured under the watchful eye of Leaf & Logic's advanced technology, whether you're present or away.


    • Automatically adjusts temperature for optimal plant health.
    • Turns on fan when tempurature is too warm.
    • Is set to know what the optimal tempurate range is for your plants.
