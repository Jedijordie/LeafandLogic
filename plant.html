Plant Care Information

Our Plants

Greenhouse Collection | Family: Peperomia

Plant varieties & starting sizes:

  • Rubella Peperomia — 4" tall, 4.5" wide
  • Peperomia Hope — 5" tall, 6" wide
  • Peperomia Marble — 6.5" tall, 9" wide
  • Cupid Peperomia — 6" tall, 9" wide

Temperature

Sensor readings in °C | Optimal Range: 18°C – 29°C

Key details:

  • Optimal temperature to maintain for plants: 18°C – 29°C
  • ±0.5°C accuracy from -10°C to +85°C
  • Uses 1-Wire interface — requires only one digital pin for communication
  • 9 to 12 bit selectable resolution
  • Multiple sensors can share one pin
  • Temperature-limit alarm system built in
  • Query time is less than 750ms

Soil Moisture

Sensor readings in % RH | Optimal Range: 50% – 70%

Key details:

  • Optimal soil moisture to maintain: 50% – 70%
  • Irrigation is automatically triggered when moisture drops to 40% – 45%
  • Uses capacitive sensing — more accurate and corrosion-resistant than resistive sensors
  • Operating voltage: DC 3.3V – 5.5V
  • Compatible with DFRobot 3-pin Gravity interface
  • Suitable for garden plants, moisture detection, and intelligent agriculture

LED Light Patterns

Full Spectrum Grow Lights | Duration: 12 – 16 hrs/day

Key details:

  • Colour combination: Red + Blue for optimal plant growth
  • Angled lighting promotes equal height and width growth
  • Brightness set to moderate intensity: 40% – 50%
  • Timer set to 12 – 16 hours per day with automatic memory cycle
  • Can be indirect lighting — does not need to be direct on the plant
  • 3 available lighting modes: Warm White, Red+Blue, and Warm White+Blue+Red
  • 5 dimming levels available from 20% – 100%

Musical Stimulation

Jazz & Classical | 1 – 3 hrs, Morning to Early Afternoon

Key details:

  • Optimal genre: Jazz or Classical music
  • Optimal frequency range: 115 – 220Hz
  • Played in the morning to early afternoon only
  • Duration: 1 – 3 hours per day at moderate volume
  • Music is not played at night — this is the plant's resting time
  • Playing music too loud can cause negative effects on plant growth
