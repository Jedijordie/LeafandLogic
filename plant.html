House Plant Information

Care Instructions for Common Houseplants:

| Ideal Temperature, Soil Moisture & LED Light Patterns

    Here is a breakdown of similar care instructions for various common houseplant families:


    Peperomias
  • Temperature: 18-29°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 50-70%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 40-45%
  • LED Light Pattern: Combination of Red and Blue; 12-16hrs; Angled Lighting; Intensity: 40-50%
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Hoyas
  • Temperature: 18-29°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 50-70%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 40-45%
  • LED Light Pattern: Combination of Red and Blue; 12-16hrs; Angled Lighting; Intensity: 40-50%
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Palms
  • Temperature: Varies by species, generally 18-29°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal varies by species; generally moderate to high; Irrigation Trigger Point: 40-50%
  • LED Light Pattern: Generally prefers indirect sunlight; 12-16hrs; Intensity: Varies by species
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Ferns
  • Temperature: Varies by species, generally 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal varies by species; generally high; Irrigation Trigger Point: 50-60%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect sunlight preferred; 12-14hrs; Intensity: Moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Succulents
  • Temperature: Varies by species, generally 18-29°C
  • Soil Moisture: Low to moderate; Irrigation Trigger Point: 20-30%
  • LED Light Pattern: Direct sunlight preferred; 10-14hrs; Intensity: Moderate to high
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Snake Plants
  • Temperature: 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 30-40%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 20-25%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect to moderate light preferred low light conditions; 10-14hrs; Intensity: Low to moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Spider Plants
  • Temperature: 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 40-60%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 30-35%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect to moderate light preferred low light conditions; 10-14hrs; Intensity: Low to moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Peace Lillies
  • Temperature: 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 60-80%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 50-55%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect to moderate light preferred low light conditions; 10-14hrs; Intensity: Low to moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • ZZ Plants
  • Temperature: 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 30-40%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 20-25%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect to moderate light preferred low light conditions; 8-12hrs; Intensity: Low to moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz

    • Philodendrons
  • Temperature: 18-24°C
  • Soil Moisture: Optimal 40-60%; Irrigation Trigger Point: 30-35%
  • LED Light Pattern: Indirect to moderate light preferred low light conditions; 10-14hrs; Intensity: Low to moderate
  • Musical Stimulation: Jazz or Classical; 1-3hrs early afternoon; Moderate volume; Hertz: 115-220Hz
